News

First created in the 1980s rush of independent game developers, this basic-code game is now being adapted for modern platforms. Inspired by popular resource-management and space-combat titles, Final Odyssey is a true staple of its time. With the gracious digital donation of its previous developer, Third Party Applications is working to show you a forgotten gem of the past. If you would like to see more updates on the restoration of this project, we invite you to follow our social media at the bottom of the page.

Our Mission

At Third Party Applications, we push the boundaries of game development to unite forgotten media with the modern age. As an independent collective passionate about creativity, code, and history, we work to ensure no project of the past will be lost to time.
