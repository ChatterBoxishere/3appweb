Restoration Initiative

Many dreams have found themselves forgotten on the back shelves collecting dust. Unfinished digital entertainment is one of the many physical remnants of this. Examples of these are game files that never surpassed the initial development phase, or even DVDs or floppy discs of now outdated aspirations. At Third Party Applications, our Restoration Initiative strives to bring those shelved concepts to the forefront of modern games. With the generous donations of our patrons, we collect and archive old code. Not simply to preserve it, like a museum, but rather to breathe new life into it for current and future generations.

FINAL ODYSSEY

The universe is bright, but vacant. Humanity has spread across the stars, building up a new future from a tarnished past. Yet we have found no signs of life in our explorations. Until now.

The greatest sin of humankind has returned in a new form: War. An unknown force, dubbed simply the enemy, has commandeered the ancient relics of man’s war machines. Now they pillage and conquer what worlds we have cultivated life from. At the same time, a newly discovered world has brought about a dark, forgotten secret.

As captain of a freighter ship, you’ve been hired once again to deliver cargo across the stars. This task comes with one requirement; You must never open the cargo. Your journey can only end when you deposit this secret into a distant black hole, destroying it forever.

You collect and hire your own personal crew and set off on your journey. The enemy, however, seems to have caught wind. Now you race against time in the hopes this will be the last act to keep history from repeating, and leave the universe empty once more.
